Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Download Embliss from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android), then sign up using your email, phone number, Google account, or Apple ID. Follow the onboarding prompts to set up your profile.

Is Embliss free?

Yes! Embliss offers a Free tier with essential features including mood tracking, basic journaling, medication reminders, and crisis resources. Premium tiers (Plus $9.99/mo and Platinum $29.99/mo) unlock additional features.

What's included in the beta program?

Beta testers (first 1,000 signups before December 31, 2025) receive FREE Platinum access during beta plus 50% off for life afterward. This discount is applied automatically‚Äîno code needed!

Account & Billing

How do I upgrade to a paid tier?

In the app, go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription ‚Üí Upgrade. Choose Plus or Platinum, select monthly or annual billing, and complete checkout through Stripe (or App Store/Google Play if applicable).

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial months.

Can I get a refund?

All sales are final. However, if you believe you were charged in error or experienced technical issues, contact support@emblisswellness.com within 7 days of your charge.

How do I update my payment method?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription ‚Üí Manage Billing. This will open the Stripe Customer Portal where you can update your card, view invoices, and manage your subscription.

Privacy & Security

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! Your journal entries, mood logs, and health data are encrypted both in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. We never sell your personal health information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Is Embliss HIPAA compliant?

We are actively working toward HIPAA compliance for future features involving licensed healthcare providers. Current wellness features follow industry-standard security practices.

Who can see my journal entries?

Your private journal entries are completely private‚Äîonly you can see them. Community forum posts are public. AI chatbot conversations are encrypted and used only to provide personalized support.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. This permanently deletes your profile, journal entries, mood logs, and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

Features & Functionality

What's the difference between Plus and Platinum?

Plus ($9.99/mo): Unlimited journaling, AI chatbot, meditation library, analytics, habit tracking
Platinum ($29.99/mo): Everything in Plus + therapist marketplace access, 1:1 coaching, priority support

How does the AI wellness companion work?

Our AI chatbot provides 24/7 emotional support, coping strategies, and wellness tips. It learns from your mood patterns and journal entries to offer personalized guidance. It's NOT a replacement for professional therapy.

Can I use Embliss offline?

Yes! You can track moods, write journal entries, and log medications offline. Your data will sync automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

Does Embliss integrate with Apple Health or Google Fit?

Yes! Plus and Platinum users can sync wellness data with Apple Health (iOS) or Google Fit (Android). Enable this in Settings ‚Üí Integrations.

Technical Issues

The app won't load or keeps crashing. What should I do?
  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Check for app updates in the App Store or Google Play
  3. Restart your device
  4. Uninstall and reinstall the app (your data is saved in the cloud)
  5. If issues persist, contact support@emblisswellness.com with your device model and OS version
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within 5 minutes.

My notifications aren't working.

iOS: Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Embliss ‚Üí Enable "Allow Notifications"
Android: Go to Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Embliss ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Enable notifications

Community & Safety

How do I report inappropriate content or users?

In the community forum, tap the "..." menu on any post or comment and select "Report." Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. For urgent safety concerns, email support@emblisswellness.com immediately.

Can I block or mute other users?

Yes. Go to a user's profile, tap "..." and select "Block" or "Mute." Blocked users cannot see your content or contact you. Muted users' posts won't appear in your feed.
