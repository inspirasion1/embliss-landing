Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 29, 2025

Welcome to Embliss Wellness ("Embliss," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Important: Embliss is designed to support mental wellness and is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and password
  • Profile Information: Optional profile photo, bio, location (city/state), and preferences
  • Health & Wellness Data: Mood entries, journal entries, medication tracking data, habit tracking, wellness goals, and AI chatbot conversations
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our community forums, direct messages, support tickets, and feedback
  • Payment Information: Billing information processed through Stripe (we do not store full credit card numbers)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, click patterns, and navigation paths
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, timestamps, and pages visited
  • Location Data: General location (city/state) based on IP address (we do not collect precise GPS location)

Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media: If you connect via Google or Apple Sign-In, we receive basic profile information
  • Health Apps: With your permission, we may integrate with Apple Health or Google Fit

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

To Provide Our Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide mood tracking, journaling, and wellness features
  • Generate personalized insights and analytics
  • Facilitate AI wellness companion interactions
  • Enable community features and direct messaging

To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Conduct research and development for new features
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Personalize content and recommendations

To Communicate With You

  • Send service updates and security alerts
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Request feedback and testimonials

For Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect user safety and wellbeing
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal health data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

With Your Consent

  • When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
  • When you participate in community forums (content is visible to other users)

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Stripe: Payment processing (they handle credit card data, not us)
  • OpenAI: AI chatbot functionality (conversations are anonymized)
  • ConvertKit: Email communications
  • Google Cloud / Apple: App hosting and delivery

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

Business Transfers

If Embliss is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

Aggregated Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you personally for research, analytics, or marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Encrypted Storage: Sensitive data (journal entries, health data) is encrypted at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your data internally
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • HIPAA Compliance: We are working towards HIPAA compliance for future features

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Access and Update

You can access and update your account information anytime through the app settings.

Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format by contacting us at privacy@emblisswellness.com.

Delete Your Data

You can delete your account and data through Settings → Account → Delete Account. Please note:

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes
  • Anonymized data used in aggregated analytics may be retained

Opt-Out of Marketing

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or updating your preferences in app settings.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

6. Children's Privacy

Embliss is available to users ages 13 and older. Users ages 13-17 have restricted access:

  • Limited to Free tier features only
  • Cannot make payments without parental consent
  • Additional privacy protections under COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Embliss is based in the United States. If you access our services from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. or other countries where our service providers operate. By using Embliss, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., crisis hotlines, external resources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Personal identifiable information is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized data may be retained for analytics and research
  • We may retain certain information as required by law (e.g., payment records for tax purposes)

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying an in-app notification

Your continued use of Embliss after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
