Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 29, 2025

Welcome to Embliss Wellness ("Embliss," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Important: Embliss is designed to support mental wellness and is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account and use Embliss, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, date of birth, password, phone number (optional)
  • Profile Information: Avatar, bio, preferences, notification settings
  • Health & Wellness Data: Mood logs, journal entries, medication tracking, habit tracking, goals, wellness scores
  • Communication Data: Messages to our support team, community forum posts, AI chatbot conversations
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full payment card information)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our app, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent in app, screen views, taps and interactions
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, crash reports, performance data
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (we do not track precise GPS location)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Authentication Providers: Google, Apple (name, email, profile picture when you use social login)
  • Health Apps: Apple Health, Google Fit (only with your explicit permission)
  • Payment Processors: Stripe (subscription and transaction details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Services: Enable mood tracking, journaling, medication reminders, habit tracking, community forums, and AI wellness companion
  • Personalize Your Experience: Generate wellness insights, recommend features, tailor AI responses based on your context
  • Process Payments: Manage subscriptions, process transactions, prevent fraud
  • Communicate with You: Send account notifications, wellness reminders, product updates, support responses
  • Improve Our Services: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Ensure Safety: Detect crisis situations, provide emergency resources, moderate community content
  • Comply with Laws: Respond to legal requests, protect our rights, enforce our Terms of Service

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Embliss will NEVER sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted service providers who help us operate:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Supabase (database hosting and authentication)
  • Payment Processing: Stripe (subscription management and secure payments)
  • AI Services: OpenAI (wellness companion chatbot responses - zero data retention policy)
  • Email Delivery: Resend, ConvertKit (transactional emails and newsletters)
  • SMS Notifications: Twilio or OpenPhone (medication/habit reminders - opt-in only)
  • Analytics: Minimal anonymous analytics for app improvement

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to:

  • Prevent imminent harm to you or others
  • Protect our legal rights and property
  • Investigate fraud or security incidents
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3.4 Business Transfers

If Embliss is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share information with other parties when you explicitly consent, such as:

  • Sharing wellness progress with a therapist you book through our marketplace (Platinum tier, future feature)
  • Connecting with accountability partners
  • Exporting data to other health apps

4. Data Security & Encryption

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data (journal entries, private messages) is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data
  • Secure Authentication: Password hashing, optional two-factor authentication
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing monitoring and vulnerability assessments
HIPAA Compliance Status: Embliss is in the process of becoming HIPAA-compliant. We use encryption and security best practices, but full HIPAA certification is planned for 2026. If HIPAA compliance is critical for you, please contact us at privacy@emblisswellness.com for updates on our certification status.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

5.1 Access & Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Export your data in PDF or JSON format
  • Access your data anytime through Settings → Privacy & Data

5.2 Correction

  • Update your profile information in-app
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data by contacting support@emblisswellness.com

5.3 Deletion

  • Delete your account and all associated data in Settings → Account Settings → Delete Account
  • Request deletion by emailing privacy@emblisswellness.com
  • All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

5.4 Opt-Out Rights

  • Marketing Emails: Unsubscribe via link in any marketing email
  • Push Notifications: Disable in Settings → Notifications or device settings
  • SMS Notifications: Reply STOP to any text message or disable in Settings
  • AI Chatbot: Stop using the feature at any time (conversations are saved unless deleted)

5.5 Restrict or Object to Processing

  • Request restrictions on how we process your data
  • Object to certain data uses (e.g., analytics)
  • Contact privacy@emblisswellness.com to exercise these rights

6. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement: Embliss requires users to be at least 13 years old.

6.1 Users Ages 13-17

  • Minors (13-17) can create free accounts
  • Paid subscriptions require parent/guardian approval
  • Parents can request access to or deletion of their child's data
  • We do not knowingly collect more data from minors than necessary

6.2 Parental Controls

Parents or guardians can:

  • Review their child's account information
  • Request deletion of their child's account
  • Revoke consent for data collection
  • Contact us at privacy@emblisswellness.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Most data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data (e.g., transaction records) retained for 7 years to comply with tax and financial laws
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, de-identified data may be retained indefinitely for research and analytics

8. SMS Text Messaging Privacy

8.1 Opt-In Required

We will only send you SMS text messages if you explicitly opt-in by:

  • Providing your phone number during account setup
  • Checking the box to consent to SMS notifications
  • Enabling SMS reminders in Settings → Notifications

8.2 Types of SMS Messages

We may send you SMS messages for:

  • Medication reminders
  • Habit tracking reminders
  • Wellness check-in prompts
  • Account security alerts
  • Critical app updates

Message frequency varies based on your notification preferences. You may receive up to 4 messages per week.

8.3 Opt-Out

You may opt-out of SMS notifications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message
  • Disabling SMS in Settings → Notifications
  • Emailing support@emblisswellness.com
IMPORTANT: SMS DATA PRIVACY PROTECTION

All personal information categories described in this Privacy Policy exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent. Your phone number and SMS consent information will not be shared with any third parties except our SMS service provider (Twilio/OpenPhone) solely for the purpose of delivering your requested messages. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your phone number or SMS opt-in data with marketers, advertisers, or any other third parties.

8.4 Carrier Charges

Message and data rates may apply. Embliss is not responsible for charges from your mobile carrier.

8.5 Help

For SMS support, reply HELP to any message or email support@emblisswellness.com
