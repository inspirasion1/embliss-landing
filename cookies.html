Cookie Policy

Effective Date: October 29, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Embliss Wellness ("Embliss," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our mobile application and website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our services, and improve your experience.

Types of Technologies We Use

  • Cookies: Text files stored in your browser
  • Local Storage: Data stored in your browser or app
  • Session Storage: Temporary data that expires when you close the app
  • Mobile Identifiers: Device identifiers used in mobile apps

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the app to function and cannot be disabled:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in securely
  • Security: Protect against fraudulent activity
  • Session Management: Remember your preferences during a session

Performance & Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how users interact with Embliss:

  • Usage Analytics: Track which features are most used
  • Error Tracking: Identify and fix technical issues
  • Performance Monitoring: Measure app speed and responsiveness

Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your choices:

  • Preferences: Remember your settings (theme, language, etc.)
  • Saved Data: Keep forms partially filled
  • Personalization: Tailor content to your interests

5. Mobile App Data Storage

In addition to cookies, our mobile app stores data locally on your device:

Local App Storage

  • User Preferences: App settings, theme, notifications
  • Cached Data: Temporary storage for faster loading
  • Offline Data: Mood logs, journal entries saved while offline
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure tokens to keep you logged in

iOS-Specific Storage

  • UserDefaults for preferences
  • Keychain for secure data (auth tokens, encryption keys)
  • SQLite database for local data caching

Android-Specific Storage

  • SharedPreferences for settings
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data
  • SQLite database for local data caching

6. How to Manage Cookies

In the Embliss App

You can control certain cookie preferences in the app:

  1. Open Embliss
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy & Data
  3. Toggle "Analytics & Performance Data" on or off

Note: Disabling essential cookies will prevent the app from functioning properly.

On iOS Devices

  1. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security
  2. Select Tracking
  3. Toggle "Allow Apps to Request to Track" off
  4. Or disable tracking per-app under "Apps That Requested to Track"

On Android Devices

  1. Go to Settings → Google → Ads
  2. Toggle "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  3. Or reset your Advertising ID

Web Browser Settings

If accessing Embliss via web browser, you can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

7. Do Not Track Signals

Our app does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" (DNT) browser signals. However, you can control tracking through the app settings as described above.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect cookies or tracking data from children under 13. Users ages 13-17 have additional privacy protections, including:

  • Limited data collection
  • No targeted advertising

9. International Users

European Economic Area (EEA)

Under GDPR, you have additional rights regarding cookies:

  • Right to consent before non-essential cookies are set
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to access information about cookies we use

California Residents

Under CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect via cookies
  • Opt-out of the "sale" of your information (we do not sell data)
  • Request deletion of data collected via cookies

10. Data Retention

Cookie data is retained for different periods depending on the type:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close the app
  • Authentication Cookies: Deleted after 7 days or when you log out
  • Preference Cookies: Retained until you clear app data or uninstall
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymized after 26 months

11. Security

We implement security measures to protect cookie data:

  • Cookies are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Authentication tokens are encrypted and securely stored
  • We use HttpOnly and Secure flags to protect against attacks
  • Regular security audits of our cookie practices
